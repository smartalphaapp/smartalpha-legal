Welcome to SmartAlpha, the mobile application developed by SmartAlpha ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("user", "you", "your"). When you use SmartAlpha, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Identifiers: Your Firebase Authentication user ID (UID).

  • Diagnostic Data: Error logs and function call metrics for reliability, debugging, and fraud prevention.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable laws.
  • Improve and personalize the user experience within the app.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data only in the following cases:

  • With trusted third-party service providers such as Google Firebase and Apple for authentication, subscription processing, and analytics, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect our legal rights, property, or the safety of users.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. All connections use HTTPS encryption, and sensitive data is stored securely using industry-standard methods.

Children’s Privacy

SmartAlpha is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from minors. If you believe that a child has provided us personal data without consent, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

Your Rights

  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Withdraw consent or object to specific types of data processing where applicable.
